Our Services

Comprehensive care for mothers & newborns — tailored, safe and compassionate.

Normal & Cesarean Deliveries

High-standard labour rooms with experienced obstetricians and painless delivery options.

Antenatal & Postnatal Care

Regular checkups, ultrasounds, counseling and post-delivery recovery support.

Gynecology & Fertility Treatments

Expert care for women's health and infertility management including IVF consultations.

Neonatal ICU (NICU)

Modern NICU with thermal control, ventilators and trained neonatal staff for critical newborn care.

Nutrition & Counseling

Diet plans, breastfeeding support, and mental health counseling for new mothers.

24/7 Emergency & Ambulance

Rapid response for emergencies with trained staff and ambulance support.
